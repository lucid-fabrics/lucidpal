# Premium Plans

LucidPal is free to use with full on-device AI. Paid plans unlock cloud AI, sync, and integrations.

---

## Tier Overview

| Plan | Price | What it adds |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Full on-device AI, calendar, habits, notes, health, voice, vision |
| **Starter** | $9.99/mo or $59.99/yr | 100 cloud AI credits/month, daily briefing, cloud sync |
| **Pro** | $19.99/mo or $119.99/yr | 500 credits, Gmail, Exchange, Immich, attachments, Brave Search, PDF processing |
| **Ultimate** | $29.99/mo or $179.99/yr | Unlimited credits, AI Memory, proactive alerts, health insights, smart widgets, data export, API access |

---

## Free — What's Always Free

The Free tier uses your iPhone's hardware — nothing we run, nothing we charge for.

- On-device AI — all supported model sizes
- Full calendar control (read, create, edit, delete events)
- Contacts, Reminders, Notes, Habits — local access
- Apple Health — all data types
- Location awareness, weather, ETA context
- Voice input + AirPods auto-voice activation
- Vision — analyze photos and screenshots on-device
- DuckDuckGo web search
- Siri Shortcuts, Live Activities
- Unlimited local chat history

:::tip
If a feature uses only your phone's built-in frameworks (calendar, health, camera), it will always be free.
:::

## Starter — Cloud AI + Sync

**$9.99/month or $59.99/year**

- **100 LucidPal AI credits/month** — powered by cloud AI for when the on-device model isn't enough
- **Daily briefing** — an AI-generated morning summary delivered as a push notification at your chosen time
- **Cloud sync** — notes and habits backed up and synced across reinstalls
- **Chat history sync** — your conversation history backed up to the cloud

Credits reset at the start of each calendar month. When you reach the limit, the app switches back to your on-device model automatically.

---

## Pro — Integrations + More Power

**$19.99/month or $119.99/year**

Everything in Starter, plus:

- **500 LucidPal AI credits/month**
- **Gmail** — read, search, and summarize your inbox. See [Gmail](./gmail).
- **Microsoft Exchange** — email and calendar from your Microsoft 365 or Outlook account. See [Microsoft Exchange](./microsoft-exchange).
- **Immich** — natural language search across your self-hosted photo library
- **Note attachments sync** — images, audio recordings, and PDFs backed up to the cloud (5 GB storage)
- **Brave Search** — higher-quality web search results
- **Document processing** — attach and summarize PDFs in chat
- **AI note enrichment** — automatic tagging, linking, and one-line summaries added to your notes in the background
- **Live Notes** — real-time voice transcription with speaker diarization and AI-generated summary saved as a note (300 min/month)

---

## Ultimate — AI That Knows You

**$29.99/month or $179.99/year**

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **Unlimited LucidPal AI credits**
- **AI Memory** — the AI remembers your preferences, goals, and context across every session. See your memory in Settings → AI Memory.
- **Proactive AI** — push alerts before you ask: traffic warnings, meeting prep, habit nudges, weather alerts, and focus suggestions
- **Apple Health AI insights** — weekly AI analysis of your health trends and correlations
- **Smart widgets** — home screen widgets powered by cloud AI, updated every 30 minutes
- **Full data export** — download all your notes, habits, chat history, and AI memory as Markdown and JSON
- **API access** — a personal API key to query your data and send AI messages programmatically. See [API Access](./api-access).
- **Live Notes** — 1,200 min/month (vs. 300 min on Pro)

## AI Credits

Cloud AI credits are consumed when LucidPal uses its cloud AI backend.

| Action | Credits used |
|--------|-------------|
| Standard AI message | 1 |
| Daily briefing generation | 1 |
| AI note enrichment (per note) | 1 |
| PDF document processing | 5 |
| Health AI weekly insight | 3 |

When you run out of credits, LucidPal falls back to your on-device model for the rest of the month. Your credit count is visible in **Settings → Subscription**.

---

## Managing Your Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. To upgrade, downgrade, or cancel:

1. Open **Settings** in LucidPal and tap your plan.
2. Tap **Manage Subscription** — this opens the App Store subscription management screen.

Alternatively, go to **iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**.
