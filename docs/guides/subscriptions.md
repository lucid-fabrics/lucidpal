# Subscriptions

LucidPal offers optional paid plans that unlock cloud AI features and higher usage limits.

## Plan Tiers

| Feature | Free | Starter | Pro | Ultimate |
|---------|------|---------|-----|----------|
| On-device AI inference | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Cloud AI chat | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Ability synthesis | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Cloud AI monthly limit | — | Standard | Higher | Highest |

All calendar, notes, habits, and on-device AI features remain free forever.

---

## Subscription Status

| Status | Meaning | Cloud AI access |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| `active` | Paid and current | ✓ Full access |
| `past_due` | Payment failed | ✗ Suspended until resolved |
| `canceled` | Subscription ended | ✗ No cloud access |

---

## Managing Your Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store.

1. Open **Settings → Subscription**
2. Tap **Manage Subscription**
3. Select your plan or cancel

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period after cancellation.

:::note
LucidPal cannot process refunds directly. For refund requests, contact Apple Support at `reportproblem.apple.com`.
:::

---

## What Happens If I Cancel?

| After cancellation | Behavior |
|-------------------|----------|
| Within billing period | Full access continues |
| After billing period ends | Cloud AI disabled, on-device AI unaffected |
| Subscribing again later | Full cloud access restored immediately |

Your notes, habits, calendar data, and sessions are never deleted.

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Subscription required" despite active subscription

1. Verify status in **Settings → Subscription**
2. Check that payment method is valid in App Store settings
3. Force-close and reopen LucidPal to refresh entitlement status

### Entitlement changes not applying

1. Close LucidPal completely (swipe up in app switcher)
2. Reopen the app
3. Entitlements refresh on launch
